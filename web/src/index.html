Crawl your site in seconds

Form Finder is a powerful, open-source tool that quickly crawls your website to find pages with forms. With a user-friendly interface and fast scanning.

How it works?

Crawls a given URL, discovers all links on the page, and follows them recursively. It flags and lists pages that have a form with at least two input fields and one submit button.
